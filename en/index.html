Land surveying and geodesy

Geodesy is needed when parcel boundaries, points, building axes or stakeout data must be transferred to the real site accurately rather than estimated on the ground.

  • Better decision-making for construction, reconstruction and land development.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about as-built drawings and surveying

This section reduces pre-sale friction and also supports the long-tail search queries people really type into Google.

What is an as-built drawing and when do I need one?

An as-built drawing is an accurate representation of the actual layout and dimensions of a property. It is often needed for registry processes, legalization, area clarification, subdivision, merger, interior design and property sale or valuation.

Do you provide apartment measurement across all Tbilisi districts?

Yes. The service is focused on Tbilisi and covers districts such as Vake, Saburtalo, Dighomi, Didube, Gldani, Isani, Samgori, Krtsanisi, Mtatsminda and others. Travel outside Tbilisi is also possible by agreement.

If I need land surveying, what information should I send first?

The location, cadastral code if available, the purpose of the work and the expected output are enough to start: boundary definition, stakeout, point marking, topo plan or another deliverable.

How long does apartment measurement or an as-built drawing take?

Many standard properties are processed within 24 to 48 hours after the site visit, but the final timeline depends on area, complexity, the number of auxiliary spaces and the required output format.

What is the difference between a topographic survey and standard land measurement?

Standard land measurement is more often focused on boundaries and coordinates, while a topographic survey also captures relief, elevations, existing buildings, roads, fences and other site elements that matter for design and planning.

Can I place an order if I am not currently in Tbilisi?

Yes. Many clients coordinate remotely. We can receive the request by WhatsApp or email, review documents, schedule the visit and keep you updated without requiring you to be on site.
