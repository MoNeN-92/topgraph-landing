Privacy Policy

This page explains how TopGraph collects and processes contact and project-related information when you reach us by phone, email, WhatsApp, or through the website quote form.

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Information We Collect

  • Name, phone number, email address, property location, and related details if you provide them to us.
  • Project type, area, cadastral or site details needed to review scope and prepare a quote.
  • Additional information shared through WhatsApp, email, or phone communication.

How We Use Information

  • To prepare pre-quotes, service offers, and project scope reviews.
  • To confirm access, location, timeline, and execution details.
  • To communicate with you about appointments, delivery, and follow-up.

Access and Retention

Access is limited to TopGraph personnel or service providers directly involved in communication, website operation, or service delivery. We retain information for as long as reasonably required for inquiries, active work, accounting, or legal obligations.

Your Rights

  • You may request updates or corrections to your contact details.
  • You may request deletion of unnecessary information where legally permitted.
  • You may ask how your submitted information is being used.

Third-Party Platforms

Communication may take place through third-party platforms such as WhatsApp or email providers. Their own policies and security terms may also apply when those channels are used.
